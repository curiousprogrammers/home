About Us




Curious Programmers, a coding platform for children between ages 8 to 24 years. They learn aspects of coding through a gamification process, which ensures we bring in an element of fun in learning. We introduce computer programming languages like Python, JavaScript and HTML in a way that is not too daunting for the young minds to grasp

Life Values




We at Curious Programmers have made it a pursuit to not only engage in analytical thinking but aim to draw attention to the softer, interpersonal traits too. We encourage students to partake in the coding process with attention to life skills such as showing respect, being empathetic and helping each other succeed. Lessons of caring, happiness, teamwork and self-exploration are important offshoots in this process of learning.

Atmost Attention




We recognize that each child is a seed of unlimited potential and as parents, teachers and caregivers, it is important we provide the right environment for them to explore opportunities, flex their creative muscles and feel free to choose their own paths.

Diversity




We run intra-country programming sessions where different students from different countries works in team to solve the use cases that enables them to understand each other perspectives and thinking process.

Happier Learning Environment




How beautiful would it be for children to grow up mindful of their own innate capacity to forge their own paths, armed with confidence in their strengths and the unique value they each bring to make this world a better place. At Curious Programmers we believe this can be a reality. Together, we contribute to a happier world!

Let the fun ride begin




Curious Programmers is for any kid between ages 8 to 24. We teach Real text-based coding, and they will have hands-on coding experience using popular languages like Python and JavaScript. We also promote soft skills like caring, helping each other succeed, working in a team and building resilience as part of our sessions. We are currently operating in six countries across the world .
